Employee Time Clock

Over- and undertime

Employee Time Clock calculates over- and undertime based on attendances and contracts/time schedules. Over- and undertime is displayed and stored in employees timesheets. The manager see the current account balance of the timesheet and the overall account balance from beginning as well. Employees leaves are considered as a full-day leave, regardless of the time, in the overtime calculation and subtracted from the duty hours.

Timesheet generation

For convenience, this screen gives a manger the possibility to generate multiple timesheets for all employees with a certain tag. If you wanna track over- and undertime it is recommended to generate timesheets for the whole year in advanced, usually on a monthly basis.

Import leaves

If you track under- and over-time its important to ignore public holidays in the calculation. This screen allows you to upload public holidays (or other leaves) by an excel file (1 column with dates), which are imported as approved leave requests for employees with a certain tag.

Odoo time clock (iPad & iPhone)

This module is well integrated with our iPhone and iPad apps, but in any case, it works without the mobile apps as well. The same for the apps, they work without the module too, but without the overtime feature.
Odoo time clock gives the employees the possibility to sign-in/sign-out with a 2D barcode card on the entrance of your company where the iPad is mounted.

Sign in/out with 2d barcode

An employee can sign in/out with holding his 2d code card to the front cam. The check-in process automatically detects if the user is currently signed in or out and executes the following step. The employee gets a quick overview of his over- and undertime total. Employees don't even need to have an odoo user.

Producing the 2d cards is easy and without additional costs

You can export your employees data from odoo as excel (name and id) and create the 2d barcode stickers by yourself with your printer. Use e.g. the Herma online tool for all herma sticker formats: Herma online tool. The tool allows you to create your desired design inclusive logo and generate a pdf for printing.

Lunch order

If Lunch order is installed and lunch items are defined, they are shown after each sign in process and the user can choose his favorite lunch.

Company info message

The company can define company wide information messages as a calender entry, tagged with "Station" and the description of the entry is shown on the time clock app for the desired period. The calender entry has to be created with the same user as used for the app.
Your company logo, if available, is displayed too.

Odoo HR (iPhone)

Odoo HR let your employees execute the most important task mobile, like sign in/out, make leave requests and see a history of attendances.

Attendance history

The employee can get a list of each attendance sign in/out for each month.

Attendance analysis

The employee can display an under- and overtime analysis for each timesheet. He see his duty hours based on his contract and his attendances hours and the diff (over- and undertime). This feature is only available, if Odoo module "Employee time clock" is installed.

Leave requests

The employee can enter leave requests.
